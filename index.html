Welcome

Manavi Associates have vast experience in the field of ESI, PF and Payroll Processing. We have a team of employees well versed and proficient to attend these jobs. In the field of Labor Record, Employee's Provident Fund (EPF), Employee's State Insurance Corp. (ESIC) maintenance. Record maintainer for various Labour Laws i.e. Employees Provident Fund, Payroll Processing & Miscellaneous Provisions Act, Employees State Insurance Act. Complete services for Factories Act. 1948, Payment of Wages under Minimum Wages Act, and Bonus Act.
