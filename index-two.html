Welcome

PaperCorp have vast experience in the field of ESI, PF and Payroll Processing. We have a team of employees well versed and proficient to attend these jobs. In the field of Labor Record, Employee's Provident Fund (EPF), Employee's State Insurance Corp. (ESIC) maintenance. Record maintainer for various Labour Laws i.e. Employees Provident Fund, Payroll Processing & Miscellaneous Provisions Act, Employees State Insurance Act. Complete services for Factories Act. 1948, Payment of Wages under Minimum Wages Act, and Bonus Act. We are in this field for last 7 years and are associated with a number of industries such as Software Companies, Educational Institutions like Schools, Colleges, Industries like Engineering, Electronics & I.T., Hospitals and Nursing Homes, Automobiles, Builders & Developers, Pharmaceuticals, Hotels, etc., located in and around Hyderabad. We take care of all your PF and ESI issues of your organisation.
